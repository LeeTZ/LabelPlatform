Welcome to Data Labling Platform. Now you are going to finish a label task for Fashion Room. This is a project using state-of-the-art deep learning technique, your work is greatly needed and appreciated to help us train our model better!

You shall find the shoulders of models. Label from left to right, sequencely, by marking left edge of shoulder, left middle shoulder, left neck, right neck, right middle shoulder and right edge of shoulder.
